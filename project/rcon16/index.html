ABU Robocon 2016

The objective of the pan-Asia contest was to design and manufacture an autonomous robot (Eco robot) and a semi-autonomous robot (Hybrid robot) capable of working in coordination to complete a particular set of tasks, where Eco robot equipped with only a single actuator for steering was guided by driving force (wind energy) obtained from Hybrid robot. We secured 3rd position among 108 participants at Nationals and were felicitated by SnT council, IIT Kanpur.
